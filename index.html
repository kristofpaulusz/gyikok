Description

Lizard Rps is a rock-paper-scissor game based on the mating behaviors of the Common side-blotched lizard. This breed of lizards have three different color variants and every color employs different strategies while competing for mating opportunities. Coincindentally this is remarkably similar to a rock-paper-scissor game. For further information see this video.

Rules:

The rules are very simple:

  • Orange beats blue
  • blue beats yellow
  • yellow beats Orange
...Have fun!
